Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How long is each session?

Sessions are available in 30, 60, 90, or 120-minute durations. We recommend at least 60 minutes for a comprehensive full-body treatment. Shorter sessions are ideal for targeted work on specific areas. Each session includes a brief pre-treatment consultation at no extra charge.

How does the mobile service work?

We travel to your home, hotel room, Airbnb, or workplace within our service area. Simply book your session, confirm your address, and we arrive fully equipped with a professional massage table, fresh linens, oils, and everything needed. A mobile service fee of $20–$50 applies based on distance.

What is the $50 booking fee?

A non-refundable $50 booking fee is required at the time of scheduling to secure your appointment slot. This fee is deducted from your total session cost — it's not an extra charge. It simply ensures commitment on both sides and protects against last-minute cancellations.

What is the cancellation policy?

We request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice will forfeit the booking fee. In exceptional circumstances, we are always happy to work with you on rescheduling. We value your time and ask that you value ours equally.

What should I wear / bring?

You will be professionally draped with fresh linens throughout the session, with only the area being worked on exposed. Clients typically undress to their comfort level. Please arrive clean, well-hydrated, and avoid eating a heavy meal immediately before your session.

Are there any health conditions I should disclose?

Yes — it is important to inform your therapist of any medical conditions, recent injuries, surgeries, medications, or areas of sensitivity before your session. This information is strictly confidential and allows us to tailor the treatment safely and effectively to your needs.

How often should I book sessions?

For general wellness and stress management, once or twice a month is ideal. Those managing chronic pain, athletic training, or rehabilitation may benefit from weekly sessions. Your therapist will provide a personalized recommendation after your first consultation.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are available for any session type or monetary value — a perfect wellness gift for a loved one. Contact us to arrange a digital or physical gift certificate. They never expire and can be used for any service we offer.
